Welcome to OpenAUD

Purpose of this Resource

This educational platform is designed to support Audiology students in low and middle income countries. Our goal is to provide accessible, comprehensive information about audiological assessment and management.

Interactive Learning

Engage with interactive diagrams and simulations to enhance your understanding of audiological concepts.

Comprehensive Coverage

Learn about the complete audiological test battery, from basic procedures to advanced diagnostic techniques.

Clinical Application

Understand how to interpret test results and develop appropriate management strategies.

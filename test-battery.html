Audiological Test Battery

Otoscopy

Otoscopy is a visual examination of the ear canal and tympanic membrane using an otoscope.

Key Points:

  • External ear canal examination
  • Tympanic membrane assessment
  • Identification of abnormalities

Tympanometry

Tympanometry measures the mobility of the tympanic membrane and middle ear system.

Key Measurements:

  • Middle ear pressure
  • Compliance
  • Ear canal volume

Pure Tone Audiometry

Pure tone audiometry determines hearing thresholds across different frequencies.

Testing Includes:

  • Air conduction
  • Bone conduction
  • Masking procedures

Speech Audiometry

Speech audiometry assesses the ability to hear and understand speech.

Components:

  • Speech Recognition Threshold (SRT)
  • Word Recognition Score (WRS)
  • Most Comfortable Level (MCL)
